Why choose Zericlean

Your trusted laundry and dry cleaning partner in Embakasi, Nairobi. We combine modern equipment with expert care to deliver exceptional results every time.

State-of-the-Art Equipment

We invest in the best commercial washing and drying equipment to ensure your clothes receive professional-grade care. Our industrial machines can handle everything from delicate fabrics to heavy-duty items with precision and efficiency.

Each load is carefully monitored for optimal washing temperature, detergent levels, and cycle duration to protect your garments while achieving exceptional cleanliness.

  • High-capacity commercial washers
  • Energy-efficient drying systems
  • Temperature-controlled washing
  • Gentle on all fabric types

Professional Steam Pressing

Our professional steam pressing service ensures your clothes look crisp and professional. We use industrial-grade steam irons and pressing equipment to achieve perfect results on shirts, dresses, suits, and all your formal wear.

Every garment is hand-pressed by our skilled team who understand the specific requirements of different fabrics and styles, ensuring your clothes always look their absolute best.

  • Professional steam pressing technology
  • Wrinkle-free guarantee
  • Collar and cuff perfection
  • Safe for delicate fabrics

Expert Wash & Fold Service

Perfect for families and busy professionals, our wash and fold service handles all your everyday laundry needs. We sort, wash, dry, and expertly fold your clothes so they come back fresh, clean, and neatly organized.

From t-shirts and jeans to towels and bed linens, we treat every item with care and attention to detail, using premium detergents that are tough on stains but gentle on fabrics.

  • Sorted by color and fabric type
  • Premium eco-friendly detergents
  • Expertly folded and organized
  • Perfect for everyday wear

Duvet & Large Items Cleaning

Our commercial equipment makes it easy to professionally clean large items like duvets, comforters, blankets, towels, and bed linens. We use special wash cycles designed specifically for bulky items to ensure thorough cleaning while maintaining softness.

Your bedding will come back fresh, fluffy, and perfectly clean, giving you the comfort of knowing you're sleeping on professionally cleaned linens.

  • Deep cleaning for large items
  • Maintains fabric softness
  • Hygienic and fresh results
  • Properly dried and folded

Eco-Friendly Practices

We care about the environment as much as we care about your clothes. Our plant-based detergents are biodegradable, hypoallergenic, and tough on stains while being gentle on fabrics and the planet.

We use energy-efficient machines and water-saving technologies to minimize our environmental footprint while delivering exceptional cleaning results for our customers.

  • Biodegradable detergents
  • Energy-efficient equipment
  • Water-saving technology
  • Safe for sensitive skin
